Electroknit Dymaxion at Bowdoin College Museum of Art

02.28.2020 – I designed a workshop around Electroknix Dymaxion, a textile-based sculpture, which I developed with Cat Mazza and the Lattice20 Collective. The sculpture is a part of the exhibit Fast Fashion/Slow Art, curated by both the Corcoran Museum of GWU and the Bowdoin College Museum of Art.

The workshop was to teach a bit about the project, the concept, and how it related to the global tradition of 2-dimensional textile patterns. (and a demonstration on how to knit them)
