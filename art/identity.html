Entropic Tendencies

 This is an identity portrait that explores the ideas of entropy in everyday life, while attempting to deliver both the likeness and mood of a personal identity. The motif of photographic frames are used to indicate a chronological sense of motion. The foreground layer of chaotic ephemera are intended to give a feeling of general disorder, while the center frames may indicate a sensation of perpetual motion.
